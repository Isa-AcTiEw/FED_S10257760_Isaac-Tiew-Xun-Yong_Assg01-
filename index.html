About Us

Founded in Shenzhen China we are a company that specializes in creating affordable gaming handhelds for running mordern pc games as well as retro games.

Our wide variety and selection of gaming handhelds allows us to accomodate to any consumer's specifications and needs.

Our Vision

We believe that the future of gaming is portable gaming.

Hence, we strive to create the best portable gaming handheld system with great performance capabilities capable of achieving smooth and an enjoyable gameplay on the go. Without compromising on user centric design and great build quality.
